NightRecall Support

Request account deletion

NightRecall lets users delete their account directly in the app from Settings > Delete Account. If you no longer have app access, use the support path below.

How in-app deletion works

  1. Open NightRecall.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Your account data, study inputs, questions, review history, and sessions are deleted.
