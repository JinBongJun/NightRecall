Account Deletion Request

We respect your choice to delete your account and data. You can initiate deletion using either of the methods below.

Method 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

The fastest way to delete your account is through the app:

  1. Open NightRecall.
  2. Navigate to Settings.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion. All data will be permanently removed instantly.

Method 2: Email Request

If you have uninstalled the app or cannot access it, please send an email to our support team:

Support Email: bongjun0289@gmail.com

Please include "Account Deletion Request" in the subject line and specify your account email address. We will process your request within 48 hours.

What information will be deleted?

Upon deletion, all the following data associated with your account will be permanently removed from our servers:

  • Account profile and authentication data.
  • Study content (photos, extracted text).
  • Generated AI questions and history.
  • Study progress and streaks.
